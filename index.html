Jane Austen

The woman too modern for her time



Jane Austen was a Georgian era author, best known for her social commentary in novels including Sense and Sensibility, Pride and Prejudice, and Emma.


Novels include:

  • Pride & Prejudice
  • Sense & Sensibility
  • Persuasion
  • Emma
  • Northanger Abbey
  • Mansfield Park
